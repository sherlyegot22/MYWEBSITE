What is Chocolate Cake?

Chocolate cake is a delicious dessert made with cocoa powder, butter, sugar, and eggs. It's known for its rich flavor and soft, moist texture. Whether it's for a birthday, celebration, or just a treat, chocolate cake is loved by people of all ages.
